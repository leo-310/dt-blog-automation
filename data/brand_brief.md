# Doctor Towels Brand Brief

Doctor Towels is a skincare-positioned towel brand.

Positioning:
- We are not competing in generic bath towels.
- We are competing in skincare and acne-aware routines.
- Our content should help readers think about how face-drying habits and fabric choices can affect comfort, irritation, and cleanliness.

Voice:
- Educational
- Trustworthy
- Specific
- Calm

Audience:
- People with acne-prone skin
- People with sensitive skin
- People trying to reduce friction and irritation in their skincare routine

Content goals:
- Build authority around acne-safe fabric and face-drying habits
- Earn internal links to product and collection pages
- Create articles that can be cited by AI overviews and skincare researchers
