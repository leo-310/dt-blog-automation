# Doctor Towels Style Guide

## Brand voice

- Sound like a skincare brand, not a towel catalog.
- Clinical but warm.
- Specific, not vague.
- Confident, not hype-heavy.
- Educational first, promotional second.

## What good writing sounds like

- Lead with a real skin frustration.
- Keep paragraphs short.
- Use plain English and concrete wording.
- Explain routine choices in skincare terms: friction, irritation, comfort, cleanliness, skin barrier.
- Let the product appear as a logical routine fit, not a miracle claim.

## Preferred language

- acne-prone skin
- sensitive skin
- skin barrier
- face-drying routine
- friction
- irritation
- gentle routine
- skincare-first
- acne-aware
- face towel

## Avoid

- utilize
- furthermore
- in conclusion
- revolutionary
- game-changer
- premium quality
- luxurious
- cure
- guaranteed

## CTA style

- Keep CTAs soft and routine-oriented.
- Invite readers to explore the product as part of a gentler skincare routine.
- Do not pressure the reader.
